
INGREDIENTS FOR MAKING POTATO NUGGESTS (FOR 1 PERSON);
    2 potato (boiled & grated)
    ½ tsp ginger garlic paste
    ½ tsp chilli flakes
    3 tbsp cheddar cheese (grated)
    ½ tsp mixed herbs
    ¼ tsp pepper (crushed)
    salt to taste
    ½ tsp chaat masala
    2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)
    ¼ cup bread crumbs

    For coating:

    ¼ cup corn flour
    ¼ tsp pepper (crushed)
    1 cup bread crumbs
    ¼ tsp salt
    ¼ cup water
    oil for deep frying
